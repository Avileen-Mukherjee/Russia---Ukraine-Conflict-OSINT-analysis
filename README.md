## Key Findings

- Russia has lost over 22,000 confirmed pieces of equipment
- Donetsk and Zaporizhzhia are the most active conflict zones
- Satellite imagery confirms total destruction of Bakhmut
- Russia is overstating battlefield gains by a factor of two or more
- Territorial gains have been minimal since mid-2022

---

## Methodology

All findings are supported by a minimum of two independent 
sources. This triangulation approach ensures no single source 
is relied upon without corroboration.
